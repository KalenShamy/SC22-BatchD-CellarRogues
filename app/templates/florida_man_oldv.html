AI Predicts Florida Man Headlines

Overview

We trained a model on Florida man headlines to teach it to generate new headlines based on a user input using a GPT2 model.

Process

  • Step 1: Collect Data
  • Step 2: Pre-Processing

    As we were looking through our dataset, we found multiple spam messages and unrelated posts in our data set. We used the python library,pandas, and different python methods to remove all of the unrelated posts from the dataset.

  • Step 3: Train Model

    We used Aitextgen to fine-tune an existing GPT-2 model and then trained the model for about 20 minutes on our filtered data set.

  • Step 4: Post-Processing

    To make our project more believable we generated mugshots to go with our headlines. To do this we compiled a file of different men and woman mugshots. We had the model randomly pick a mugshot depending if the the AI generated a headline to do with Florida Man or Woman. We also used the package, Better_Profanity, to ensure that our headlines were clean. Lastly to we capitalized the main words in our headline.

  • Step 5: Deploy Product

    After creating a working model, we used Flask to deploy it.

  • Tech Stack:
    • Aitextgen
    • GPT-2
    • Flask
